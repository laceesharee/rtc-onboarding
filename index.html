Fund Contribution — How It Works

For every RTCN-dispatched appointment, 50% of the base service fee is remitted to the RTCN Business Fund. The state notary fee ($10) and all travel/mileage fees are yours in full. After-hours and holiday surcharges split 50/50. The monthly fund contribution is capped at $150 — once reached, you keep 100% of all fees for the rest of that calendar month. Jobs from your personal Snapdocs profile are entirely yours.
